Thank you for visiting Spirit Connections

My name is Cynthia and I have been blessed with the gift of connecting with the Spirit world since childhood. I would love the opportunity to help you communicate with friends and loved ones that have passed on.

Would you like to connect with a loved one you've lost? I've been given the ability to receive messages from those who've passed on and can relay those to you.

To get started, please email me at spiritconnect4@gmail.com. Include a close-up photo of the person you want to contact and describe your relationship to them. All communication is sacred and confidential. Any messages I receive will be passed on to you verbatim.

Each session is $95 per person contacted. If I am unable to receive any communication from the person you wish to contact, no payment is necessary.

Due to the nature of this medium, please be patient as it can take time. Feel free to email me with any questions.
